# Lector — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-07-05_

**Lector collects no personal data. None.**

- Lector requests **no Android permissions**.
- Lector has **no analytics, no trackers, no advertising, and no accounts**.
- Lector makes **no network connections**. Your text and documents never leave your device.
- Documents you open (files, folders, pasted or shared text) are read **on your device
  only**, through Android's system file picker; Lector does not upload, transmit, or
  store them anywhere off your device.
- Text is read aloud using **your device's own text-to-speech engine**. Lector does
  not send your text to any external service.
- Lector keeps a small local list of documents you've opened (its "library" and
  "recent" lists) and your reading position, **stored only on your device** so you can
  resume. This data never leaves the device and is removed when you uninstall the app.

Because Lector collects and transmits nothing, there is no data to access, correct,
export, or delete beyond uninstalling the app.
